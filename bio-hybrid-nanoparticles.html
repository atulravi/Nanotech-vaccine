Advancements in Bio-Hybrid Nanoparticles

Bio-hybrid nanoparticles (BHNPs) combine biological materials, such as proteins or cell membranes, with synthetic nanoparticles to enhance their functionality. These particles improve vaccine delivery, immune response stimulation, and targeted drug release.
